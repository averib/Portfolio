About Me

Hello there, my name is Averi and to describe myself in a nutshell: I'm a UX/UI designer and illustrator who loves to code. It's nice to meet you!

As an individual who has always let art and creativity drive them, there's nothing more enjoyable than exploring what I can do with design when given a problem and the tools to solve it (except maybe food and movies).

I always find excitement in creating beautiful and meaningful experiences, whether it's through digital interfaces, web pages, or art, and I'm always looking for new opportunities to build something new.
