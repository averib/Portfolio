Work with Me

2018  /  UX + UI DESIGN

Mobile application for gym enthusiasts

View Process

Overview

Work with Me is a social app that allows users who want to go to the gym with a partner or group to connect and coordinate with others who share the same goal. Along with my 2 teammates, I share the role of user experience and interface designer for the app, which is currently still in development.

Concept

The app is designed to bring together gym enthusiasts who struggle to find others to workout with using a scheduling and user messaging system. In the process of determining features, I participated in user research to learn more about our target audience and understand what drives them to go to the gym.

These interviews opened up insightful discussions regarding personal fitness goals, motivations, workout activities, and pain points, which gave us a good initial sample to represent our demographic and outline a feature plan.

UX Design

When it came to designing the UX and interaction, our team held brainstorming sessions to visualize our app's layout in relation to task flows. Using the personas we created, I also designed the experience map we needed to better understand how we want our users to go through the process of scheduling a workout and connecting with others.

Interaction

Currently, we are continuing our exploration of interaction and how we could best support the social needs of our intended users. Our primary focus is to make the act of scheduling a workout as quick and seamless as possible so that the task can easily be repeated as needed.

For this stage, I've created interactive wireframes of our core feature's UI to explore animation styles and content hierarchy.
